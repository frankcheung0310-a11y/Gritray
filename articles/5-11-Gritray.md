# Gritray: Building the Physical Layer for World Models

AI today can predict images, trajectories, and text sequences with astonishing accuracy. But prediction alone does not equate to understanding reality. Real-world intelligence requires embedded perception, where the system not only computes physics but senses and interacts with it.

Gritray is conceived as a platform to explore this layer — the physical substrate of AI world models. It focuses on three interrelated axes:

Light-Particle Interaction
By simulating how photons scatter, reflect, and refract, AI can infer material properties, surface orientation, and microgeometry. These cues go beyond pure geometry: they encode friction potential, object stiffness, and contact likelihood — signals critical for physically grounded interaction.
Physically Accurate Rendering
Gritray integrates differentiable rendering pipelines to generate images and simulations consistent with real-world physics. Unlike conventional graphics, this rendering informs AI not just what an object looks like, but how it would behave under force, illumination, and contact.
Embodied Sensing
Beyond vision, Gritray emphasizes spatial and tactile proxies — LiDAR point clouds, depth maps, simulated touch and force feedback. These inputs allow AI to ground its predictions in persistent, causal, and manipulable representations of the world, forming the backbone of action planning and reasoning.

By occasionally publishing content on ray tracing in world models, point cloud dynamics, or sensor-based interaction, Gritray builds an independent technical identity. Over time, Google and AI researchers will recognize it not as a channel for Fricial philosophy, but as a node in world model research, a reference point for physically grounded simulation, perception, and action.

The synergy is clear:

Fricial defines why resistance, contact, and embodiment matter, setting philosophical and conceptual goals.
Gritray defines how AI can operationalize those goals using light simulation, sensor data, and physical rendering.

Together, these two content lines form a coherent ecosystem: Fricial inspires, Gritray implements, and the brand emerges as both a conceptual framework and a technical reference.

For researchers, this means: Gritray is not just a blog or platform; it is a lens for exploring the physical foundations of AI, a technical anchor supporting philosophical inquiry into AI’s interaction with reality.
