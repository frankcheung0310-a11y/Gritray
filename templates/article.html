Friction: The Missing Grammar of World Models

May 9, 2026

Digital scaling ignores the primary constraint of reality: Resistance. While LLMs excel in frictionless token prediction, embodied agents must master the interaction with surfaces.

We introduce Fricial Intelligence. It is not artificial; it is grounded. It is the intelligence that understands that every action is a negotiation with friction and material grit.

For a robot to perceive a door handle, it must see past the image and feel the potential slip. This is the Gritray perspective—where sensing meets surface truth.
